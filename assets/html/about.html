Omni Notes is an open source project available at https://github.com/federicoiosue/Omni-Notes and the complete app changelog is available here

Give a look at Google+ Comunity for Beta releases and at developer's personal mini-site for other softwares, libraries and trashwares!

Translations

ON is currently translated in:

Arabic, Chinese, Dutch, French, Galician, German, Hebrew, Italian, Lao, Portugese (Brazil and Portugal), Turkish, Russian, Slovak, Spanish, Swedish, Ukrainian

The translation project itself is opened to everyone wants to help, join the community of (great) translators at Crowdin!
